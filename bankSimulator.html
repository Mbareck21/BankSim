BankSim

BankSim is a bank simulation app that lets you create and manage your own virtual bank. You can choose from different types of accounts, loans, investments, and services to offer to your customers. You can also set the interest rates, fees, and policies for your bank. You can compete with other banks in the market and see how your decisions affect your profitability, customer satisfaction, and reputation. BankSim is a fun and educational way to learn about banking and finance. Whether you want to run a small community bank or a global financial institution, BankSim gives you the opportunity to experience the challenges and rewards of being a bank manager.
